Plot how attention to a work or group moved across the journals. Add works or
groups, compare them against each other, or switch to the field comparison to
see where philosophy and classics journals diverge.

**Every line is a floor.** Each counts only the citations we could place to a
specific passage; detected-but-unplaceable traffic has no year and is absent
here entirely. Works whose page numbers collide lose more to that floor than
others, and the loss is uneven across years — so read a line's shape over time
rather than its height against another's. See
[methods](/methods/#floors-not-totals) for how citations were placed.
