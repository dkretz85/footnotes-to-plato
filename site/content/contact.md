## Features currently in planning

- **Co-citation:** which passages tend to be cited together in the same article.
- **Coverage beyond journal articles,** if the licensing can be arranged.
- **More authors** — Homer, Pindar, the Presocratics, Kant, Bible texts…
- **Aristotle by line and chapter,** rather than Bekker pagination.
